Join ACM@MC

By joining ACM@MC, you will unlock access to our network, including our Google Drive and GitHub, as well as after hours access to our lab, our coffee stash, and the ability to be an active voting member as well as hold an executive position.

The responsibilites of all active members of the Association for Computing Machinery Chapter at Monmouth College require a $30 yearly membership fee, which covers both your offcial student membership with the ACM and your dues to the local chapter. Along with dues, we ask that you be a contributing member to the organization by coming to meetings, participating in activities, and having fun!

If you are interested in becoming a part of ACM@MC, come get to know the organization by coming to meetings and events! All our events can be found on our calendar, as well as our chapter meetings, which are open to the public. Otherwise email us for more information!
