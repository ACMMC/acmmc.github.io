About ACM@MC

General Introduction

ACM@MC is a 501(c)(3) non-for-profit student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, a widely recognized organization for computing professionals, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession; enable professional development; and promote policies and research that benefit society. The ACM@MC Student Chapter was founded in February, 2014.

Full body chapter meetings and executive board meetings alternate every Wednesday night. Our full chapter meetings are open and welcoming to the public in the Center for Science and Business Networking Lab. Details on exact meeting time can be found on our Google Calendar.
