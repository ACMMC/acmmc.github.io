iOS Development Workshop

General Questions

What will I learn by the end of this workshop?

By attending this workshop we hope you gain a base understanding and enjoyement for the Swift language and iOS development! From here we hope you begin exploring all the wonderful ideas you can bring to life with Swift and iOS.

What kind of tools will I be working with?

We will be introducing you to the wonderful world of app development! You will learn how to use Apple's Xcode IDE, the Swift programming language, iPhone simulators, profiles and certificates, and iTunes Connect.

What do I need to bring?

While you don't necessarily need to bring anything, we would love for to you follow along by coding with us! For those individuals, we ask you come prepared with:

  • Either a Macbook (Pro, Air, etc)/Hackintosh/VM running OS X
  • Download Xcode Github Desktop Client
  • Download/Update Command Line Tools in the Mac App Store
