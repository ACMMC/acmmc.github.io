Join ACM@MC

If you are interested in becoming a part of ACM@MC, come get to know the organization by coming to meetings and events! All our events can be found on our calendar, as well as our chapter meetings, which are open to the public.

By joining ACM@MC, you will unlock access to our network, including our Google Drive and GitHub, as well as after hours access to our lab and the ability to hold an executive position.
